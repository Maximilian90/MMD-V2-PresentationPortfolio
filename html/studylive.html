Studieliv App design

Group project

Code prototype
In this project, we should find out which needs and wishes students have these days at UCL. It was really clear after some interviews, that they want and wish for a possibility to get in contact with other students and get informed about events and clubs at the school. There was also 8 out of 10 that didn't know about the Studieliv site on UCLs website, because it's too hidden. So our group decided to design a Studiliv app.
